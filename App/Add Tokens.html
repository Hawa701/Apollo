What are Apollo Tokens?

Apollo Tokens are our platform's digital currency, designed to simplify the payment process between clients and freelancers. By using Apollo Tokens, you can easily manage your budget, track your expenses, and streamline your transactions on our platform.

Why use Apollo Tokens?

  1. Faster transactions: Apollo Tokens allow for quicker and more efficient payments between clients and freelancers.
  2. Reduced fees: Save on transaction fees by using Apollo Tokens for all your projects.
  3. Secure payments: Our robust security system ensures that your tokens are safe and protected.
  4. Easy budget management: Keep track of your expenses by using a single currency for all your projects on the platform.
  5. Exclusive benefits: Unlock special discounts and offers available only to Apollo Token users.

How to Purchase Apollo Tokens

To purchase Apollo Tokens, follow these simple steps:

  1. Log-in to your Apollo Freelance account.
  2. Navigate to the "Token Management" section in your dashboard.
  3. Select the desired amount of tokens you'd like to purchase.
  4. Choose your preferred payment method (credit/debit card, PayPal, telebir etc.).
  5. Confirm your purchase and receive your Apollo Tokens instantly!

How to Use Apollo Tokens

Using Apollo Tokens is easy! When you're ready to make a payment on the platform, simply choose the "Pay with Apollo Tokens" option. You'll see the amount of tokens required for the transaction, and after confirming, the tokens will be transferred from your account to the freelancer's account.

How to Check Your Apollo Token Balance

To check your Apollo Token balance:

  1. Log-in to your Apollo Freelance account.
  2. Navigate to the "Token Management" section in your dashboard.
  3. Your current token balance will be displayed on the screen.
